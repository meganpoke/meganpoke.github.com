I thought this image is interesting because it provide a lot of question and space for imagination. The cat is in the middle of the forest, looking straight at your eyes, makes me wonder "what is he thinking?" The background and the foreground are all blurry, and the center of the image is sharp, in focus. This draw attention of the viewer to focus on the cat. This photo helps telling story of my photography style. There are three styles of my photography style I want to show as my collection. My topic is my photo collection of outdoor photo, studio photo, and portrait that I took. This photo will represent the outdoor photo.

This image created by Mindy is really cute. I like her illustration style, the pattern on the snake is really abstract, yet interesting. I like how the snake is looking at you, it draws the attention to the middle. I believe this image will be used as her theme of her pets.
