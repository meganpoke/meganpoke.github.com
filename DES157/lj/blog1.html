Megan's Learning Journal

Best Practice of Design

Date 1/20

This article is extremely helpful. The first thing that I have learnt was that we have to be careful and only ask the important question. Users do not want to spend time to fill out forms with too many fields on it. I also learned that one column form is faster to process than multiple columns. This was new to me because I have been doing multiple column in my old designs.

Another interesting information is that user are more likely to finish the field when they are provided with clear picture of the number steps it takes to complete.
